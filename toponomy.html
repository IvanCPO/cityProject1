Toponomy

The toponomy of Venecia is the Friuli-Venecia Julia (in italy Friuli-Venezia Giulia) and its people are friulano/-na. But this's not the unic toponomy, It have:

  • Friaul-Julisch Venetien (alemán)
  • Friûl-Vignesie Julie (friulano)
  • Furlanija-Julijska krajina (esloveno)
