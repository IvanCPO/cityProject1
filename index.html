What is Venice known for?

Venice is well known for having its streets flooded and riding in gondolas. The map itself does not have any road markings as they are water channels.

Gondola

The gondola is a traditional Venetian rowing boat. Gondolas were for centuries the main means of transportation in Venice and still play an important role, serving as transfers to and from the main canals.
